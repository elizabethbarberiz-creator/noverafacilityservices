About Novera

Professional Standards. Personal Service.

Novera Facility Services provides dependable, detail-focused commercial cleaning solutions for businesses throughout the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Story

A Cleaning Partner Built Around Trust

Novera Facility Services was created with a simple goal: to provide businesses with professional, reliable and personalized commercial cleaning services they can depend on.

We understand that every facility is different. That is why we do not believe in one-size-fits-all cleaning packages. Every client receives a customized plan based on their property, schedule, priorities and operating needs.

From offices and restaurants to retail stores, healthcare settings and commercial properties, Novera helps businesses maintain spaces that feel clean, welcoming and professionally cared for.

Our Mission

Cleaner Facilities. Stronger First Impressions.

Our mission is to help GTA businesses create healthier, more professional and more welcoming environments through dependable cleaning programs and responsive personal service.
